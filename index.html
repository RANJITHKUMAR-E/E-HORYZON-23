E-HORYZON'23 - A LOOK


“Innovation is seeing what everybody has seen and thinking what nobody has thought”

Innovation Council brings a voice of experience from the real economy that will support innovation and the diffusion of technology and knowledge over the long-term. We believe that technological innovation and collaboration among innovators will be critical to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals. 

  • To develop entrepreneurship awareness among the students.
  • To promote and create an entrepreneurial culture in the institution.
  • To provide platform for skill development and employment opportunities for students
  • The main objective of IIC is to encourage, inspire and nurture young students by supporting them to work with new ideas and transform them into prototypes.
E-Horyzon'23 is a self sustained initiative of Innovation & Entrepreneurship Forum (IEF).
            It is an intra-college event conducted by EMDC & IIC of Kongu Engineering College . The events are planned with the objective to provide platform for the students to showcase their talents and skills with a competitive spirit. This encourages the students to involve in innovation and entrepreneurship activities by taking part in various events conducted. The innovations are based on the current problem statements and solution requirements. Students can improve themselves with the platform provided and also can enhance the productivity period through participating various events conducted. Indeed, we represent this useful forum so as to appreciate innovations requires some initiation and interest .
